🌟 Story of the Day: Ronaldo Silences the Critics

After a difficult first match against Congo DR, Cristiano Ronaldo reminded the world exactly why he is still playing at the highest level. The Portuguese captain scored twice in a dominant 5-0 victory over Uzbekistan, taking his World Cup goal tally to new heights and firing Portugal to the top of their group.

Nuno Mendes and Rafael Leão also found the net as Portugal put on a masterclass of attacking football. Ronaldo's performance has once again sparked debate — is the 41-year-old still the best player at this tournament?
